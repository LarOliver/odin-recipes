Chicken Parm

Description

Chicken parm is the essential dish most people have hard of and many people love, myself included!

Ingredients

  • 1 skinless boneless chicken breast
  • 4 slices of mozarella chesse
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • Italian Braedcrumps
  • egg

Steps to cook

  1. Dip chicken in egg yolk then dip in cup of breadcrumps
  2. Over a high heat saucepan, cook the chicken for 4 minuts each side
  3. Take chicken off heat, and put mozarella cheese on top
  4. Place chicken in a baking dish with sauce
  5. Bake for 20 minutes
