Penne Vodka

Description

Penne vodka is a traditional italian american dish that is quite simple to make. My mother always made this for us growing up.

You will be able to find this dish is almost any restaurant around the globe especially in the USA

  • 1 package penne pasta
  • 2 tablesspoons butter, preferably unsalted
  • 1/4 pound thinly slices pencetta bacon chopped
  • 1/3 cup of vodka
  • 1.5 cups of tomato sauce
  • .5 cups grated parmesan cheeese

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter or margarine in a large skillet over medium heat. Add pancetta, and saute until lightly browned. Add vodka and stir until it is reduced by half, about 4 to 5 minutes. Stir in tomato sauce and cream. Simmer uncovered for 10 to 12 minutes. Stir every few minutes.
  3. Stir in pasta, and heat through. Serve with Parmesan cheese.
